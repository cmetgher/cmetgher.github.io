Hi. I'm Cristina Metgher and I am working on my PhD in Computational Social Science at the Krasnow Institute for Advanced Study, at GMU.
In my dissertation I use Agent Based Modeling and Social Network Analysis to better understand the social determinants of cancer. I have a MSc degree in Computer Science from Technical University of Moldova.
